About Me

I'm a passionate developer specializing in full-stack web development and mobile applications. I love turning complex problems into elegant solutions through clean, efficient code.

But my curiosity doesn't stop at the terminal — I'm drawn to anthropology, visual art, and the slow work of understanding how our species evolves, biologically, culturally, and technologically.

My interests span from technical architecture and scalable systems to anthropology and art. I believe in continuous learning and sharing the knowledge with others.

Growing up between cultures taught me that the best ideas live at intersections. I believe the most impactful work happens not in silos, but at the messy, beautiful crossroads of diverse fields and lived experiences.
